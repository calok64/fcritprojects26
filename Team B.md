## **Problem Statement** 

## **Background** 

While procurement workflows manage transactional activities, organizations often lack intelligence regarding inventory availability, procurement risks, supplier performance, delivery execution, and procurement trends. 

Most procurement decisions are reactive rather than proactive due to the absence of real-time insights and predictive analytics. 

This results in delayed deliveries, stock shortages, supplier issues, financial losses, and poor procurement planning. 

## **Current Business Challenges** 

## **Inventory Visibility Issues** 

Procurement teams often initiate purchases without knowing: 

- Current stock levels 

- Reserved inventory 

- Available alternatives 

- Reorder requirements 

Challenges: 

- Overstocking 

- Stock shortages 

- Duplicate procurement 

## **Procurement Risk Management** 

Organizations face risks related to: 

- Vendor reliability 

- Fraudulent transactions 

- Compliance violations 

- Financial exposure 

Current risk assessments are often manual and retrospective. 

## Challenges: 

- Delayed risk identification 

- Financial losses 

- Procurement disruptions 

## **Fulfillment Tracking Challenges** 

After approvals and contract execution, tracking procurement fulfillment becomes difficult. 

Challenges: 

- Delivery delays 

- Missed milestones 

- Poor supplier performance visibility 

- Lack of escalation mechanisms 

## **Lack of Procurement Analytics** 

Organizations struggle to answer questions such as: 

- Which vendors are performing poorly? 

- What categories have the highest spend? 

- What procurement activities carry the highest risk? 

- What orders are likely to be delayed? 

Without analytics, procurement teams cannot optimize operations effectively. 

## **Proposed Solution** 

Develop an AI-driven Procurement Intelligence & Fulfillment Platform that provides proactive visibility into inventory, risk, fulfillment, and procurement performance. 

The platform continuously analyzes procurement data and generates actionable insights for decisionmakers.
