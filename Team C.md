## **Team C – Agent Operations & Platform Engineering** 

## **Problem Statement** 

## **Background** 

As organizations adopt Agentic AI architectures, multiple AI agents operate simultaneously across procurement workflows. 

Without centralized monitoring, orchestration, governance, and scaling mechanisms, AI systems become difficult to manage and unreliable in production environments. 

Organizations require enterprise-grade operational capabilities to ensure AI agents remain scalable, observable, secure, and cost-efficient. 

## **Current Business Challenges** 

## **Limited Agent Visibility** 

Organizations cannot easily determine: 

- Which agents are running 

- Agent health status 

- Agent failures 

- Processing bottlenecks 

Challenges: 

- Operational blind spots 

- Slow incident resolution 

## **Scalability Challenges** 

Workloads fluctuate significantly. 

Examples: 

- End-of-month procurement spikes 

- Seasonal demand increases 

- Bulk document uploads 

Without dynamic scaling: 

- Processing delays occur 

- Infrastructure costs increase 

## **AI Cost Management** 

Large language model usage introduces: 

- Token consumption costs 

- Model invocation expenses 

- Infrastructure overhead 

Challenges: 

- Uncontrolled spending 

- Lack of usage visibility 

## **Governance and Compliance** 

Organizations require: 

- Auditability 

- Explainability 

- Policy enforcement 

- Access control 

Without governance: 

- Compliance risks increase 

- Regulatory requirements become difficult to satisfy 

## **Reliability Challenges** 

Agent failures can interrupt business processes. 

Examples: 

- OCR failures 

- Vendor verification failures 

- Risk model failures 

- Notification delivery failures 

Organizations need automatic recovery mechanisms. 

## **Proposed Solution** 

Develop a centralized Agent Operations & Platform Engineering Platform that provides monitoring, orchestration, governance, scalability, and operational intelligence for all AI agents. 

The platform acts as the operational backbone of the AI ecosystem.

## **Master Agent**

- Agent lifecycle management

- Workload distribution

## **Expected Business Outcomes**

- Reduced operational incidents 

- Improved scalability 

- Better AI cost control
